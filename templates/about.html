About Me

This is what I do.

Hello everyone who has visited my humble blog. My name is Misha. I love to play sports, horizontal bars, parallel bars, I also love snowboarding. I play the guitar and I don't seem to sing badly. I also developed a love for programming. As it turned out quite recently. Less than a year ago, I was offered to try. And I was overwhelmed with it. I didn’t work as a programmer, and I didn’t study for it at the university. I come from a family of railroad workers, and therefore, as probably most of you follow in the footsteps of your parents. I work as an assistant driver of a diesel locomotive, I drive trains. Programming for me now is more of a hobby, and I expect that later it will be a full-fledged job that will bring satisfaction and love. I don't want to come to work and think about when you have your next break. I want to go to work and solve interesting problems, I want to get high from work. I want to be a programmer. After all, coding me into a thrill.
